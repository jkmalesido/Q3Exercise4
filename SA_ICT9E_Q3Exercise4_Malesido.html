Calle Crisologo, Vigan




Vigan City is one of the largest factors that contribute to the country's tourism. This place, especially Calle Crisologo, is in demand to tourists around the globe due to its historical and cultural heritage. You'll be transported back in time once you visit Calle Crisologo in Vigan City. The cobble-stoned street is filled with centuries old homes dating back to the Spanish colonial era. The place is also filled with souvenir shops, coffee shops, restaurants, and many more! Only horse-drawn buggies (which the locals call 'kalesa') are allowed here. So, wear your barong tagalog/baro't saya and take that obligatory Facebook or Instagram selfie here in Calle Crisologo!
